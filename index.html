About The Game

Kingdom of Words: A 2D RPG Game for Learning French Language is an interactive educational game developed by John Albert Salazar. The purpose of this study is to develop an engaging and immersive learning experience that helps Hospitality Management students learn basic French vocabulary, phrases, and communication skills through quest-based gameplay.

Unlike traditional learning methods, Kingdom of Words combines entertainment and education into one magical adventure. Players interact with NPCs, complete story-driven missions, answer quizzes, solve challenges, and experience real-life hospitality scenarios while learning French.

The game focuses on helping learners overcome common challenges in learning French such as pronunciation, grammar rules, and unfamiliar vocabulary. Through progressive gameplay and exciting RPG mechanics, players gradually improve their communication skills while enjoying a fantasy adventure.

Game Levels

LEVEL 01

Polite Phrases

Learn polite French phrases by selecting the correct language answers during interactive quests.

LEVEL 02

Restaurant

Explore restaurant vocabulary through drag-and-drop challenges using utensils and French food terms.

LEVEL 03

Hotel Stay

Experience hotel scenarios where players check in guests and learn hospitality-related French vocabulary.

LEVEL 04

Travel & Directions

Help travelers find directions while learning travel vocabulary and communication skills in French.

LEVEL 05

Royal Tea Party

Attend a royal tea party and practice formal French phrases, manners, and respectful communication.
